Founder & Head of Operations

CA Deepesh Kingrani

Deepesh Kingrani is a Chartered Accountant with 10+ years of experience in accounting, taxation, and financial operations. As Founder and Head of Operations, he leads delivery, client onboarding, and process standardization across all engagements.

He has extensive experience working with white-label accounting firms, providing backend support for bookkeeping, reconciliations, and month-end reporting while maintaining strict confidentiality and quality standards. His work enables partner firms to scale efficiently without compromising on accuracy or turnaround time.

Deepesh specializes in international accounting and Virtual CFO services, supporting clients across the UK, US, Australia, and other global markets. He focuses on building streamlined accounting systems, ensuring clean books, and delivering actionable financial insights for business decision-making.

He also supports foreign businesses setting up in India, guiding them through entity structuring, compliance requirements, and ongoing financial management.

"His approach is practical, process-driven, and focused on delivering consistent, reliable outcomes for both direct clients and partner firms."
